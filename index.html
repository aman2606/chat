Frequently asked questions

What is Education on Demand?

Education on Demand is an online subscription-based platform hosting art-based lesson plans for teachers. Lessons are designed for student levels from foundation to secondary school, covering a range of materials, techniques, and artists and link to the Australian Curriculum. Each lesson includes streaming instructional videos with downloadable written lesson plans with step-by-step instructions, suitable for teacher planning. Videos and resources can also be shown in the classroom directly to students. Community forums support networking and feedback through question-and-answer discussion threads.
